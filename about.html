About me.

A little something if you're ever feeling bored enough read a story of a stranger, Jan 26, 2023

My name is Joshua Miller. I was born in New Zealand some number of years ago, details of which are foggy. I have recently immigrated to the US after falling madly in love with a young American girl who's name escapes me. While making bad decisions as a teen, I decided a career in winemaking would be a fun idea. I embarked on a journey to get a Bachelors Degree in Wine Science from a small region of New Zealand called Hawkes Bay. Ten years later and I have once again decided to embark on another journey. This time, it seems like a rather fun idea to delve into the world of Programming.
