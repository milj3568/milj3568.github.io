I have a passion for Winemaking. Here are some of my favorite regions

Margaret River

Hidden on the West Coast of Australia, Margaret River is as close to paradise as you can get. Home to some of the worlds oldest soils, this region utilizes a Mediterraean climate and its natural maritime influence to create some amazing wines. Notable for their elegant and luscious Cabernet Sauvignon and world class Chardonnay.

Willamette Valley

The leading wine region in Oregon, the Willamette Valley is home to more than 700 wineries. A cool climate region, boasting some of the worlds greatest Pinot Noir and gaining a reputation for delicious Chardonnay.

Hawkes Bay

The region that nurtured me as a Winemaker. Hawkes Bay is New Zealand's second largest wine region, and also the place I called home while studying Wine Science. A temperate climate and a healthy dose of sunshine, Hawkes Bay is gaining traction as a go to spot for Wine Enthusiasts. Full bodied Chardonnays and a penchant for Bordeaux-style Blends are leading the way for wineries in this region, but if you feel adventurous, look out for some of the worlds finest Pinot Gris.
